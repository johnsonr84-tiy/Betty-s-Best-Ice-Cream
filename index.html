History of Betty's

In 1997 Betty's Best Ice Cream opened it's first shop in downtown Salt lake City. It is nestled right in the heart of the city just blocks from City Creek and one of the best coding schools Utah, The Iron Yard. One of our most loyal customers is an instructor at the Iron Yard and his name is Sean Duncan.
