History of Betty's

In 1997, Betty's Best Ice Cream opened it's first shop in downtown Salt lake City. It is nestled right in the heart of the city just blocks from City Creek and one of the best coding schools Utah, The Iron Yard. One of our most loyal customers is actually an instructor at the Iron Yard and his name is Sean Duncan. He really enjoys the mint chocolate chip ice cream and we are told he takes caution when eating it around his little pups. Mr. Duncan uses our fine ice cream as power up energy to teach coding for hours a day.
